# [BetterBox](https://kidstylex.github.io/betterbox/)

![nwdn_file_temp_1615303021945](https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/47844417/110493526-46692100-8125-11eb-9726-449979cda09b.JPG)


This project is a part of Exceed Camp 16th.

In response to the spate of tragic events involving student suicide, we the Department of Computer Engineering, Kasetsart University, in collaboration with the Department of Mental Health and Psychiatric Nursing, Mahidol University, are channeling this year’s creative energy of the eXceed Camp toward harnessing the digital technology in empowering the Reachability-of-Help and Youth Mental Strengthening.

" Sleep issues may contribute to the development of depression through changes in the function of the neurotransmitter serotonin. Sleep disruptions can affect the body’s stress system, disrupting circadian rhythms and increasing vulnerability for depression. "

So we developed a web application and product called BetterBox. It can help people to contact their doctor and track sleep behavior. And also have a BetterBox product can display different colors that can evoke different emotions and help people sleep better. All of this project _finished in 24 hours_

Related blogs:

[Depression and Sleep](https://www.sleepfoundation.org/mental-health/depression-and-sleep)

[Color Psychology: The Emotional Effects of Colors](http://www.arttherapyblog.com/online/color-psychology-psychologica-effects-of-colors/#.YEeHYS2l1QI)
